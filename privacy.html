Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31st August 2024

UseGIS Limited (“UseGIS” or “we”) values your privacy. This privacy policy (the “Policy”) sets out how we collect, use and protect your data when you access, use and play games, features and services via websites or mobile apps produced by UseGIS. UseGIS, a Limited company registered in England and Wales (company number 5354108) is the controller (or a similar role) who is responsible for handling your data under this Policy.

What Data We Collect About You

We do not collect any data that can identify you individually.

We do collect non-personal data for the following purposes:

What we collect How we use it
Device ID This anonymously identifies your specific device. If you change your device, your Device ID will change.
Game data Any progress you make in a game is stored so that you can carry on where you left off the last time you played that game on that device.
Purchase data This records what purchases you have made on your device so we can track sales and provide refunds if required.

Your Legal Rights

You may have certain legal rights in relation to processing of your data, which may include:

  • Access right: you may request access to your data in our possession.
  • Rectification right: you may request us to correct your data if it is inaccurate.
  • Erasure right: you may request us to delete your data.
  • Restriction right: you may request us to restrict how we process your data.
  • Portability right: you may request us to help you transfer your data to other entities.
  • Withdrawal of consent: you may withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Right to complain and report: you have the right to complain to the relevant supervisory authority.

The above rights may only apply in limited circumstances under the applicable law and if we decide not to respond to your claim for the above rights, we will explain reasons.

Data Security

We are committed to keeping your data safe. We take reasonable and appropriate administrative, technical, organisational, and physical security and risk management measures in accordance with applicable laws to ensure that your data is adequately protected against accidental or unlawful destruction, damage, loss or alteration, unauthorised or unlawful access, disclosure or misuse, and all other unlawful forms of processing of your data in our possession.

Disclosure

We may disclose your data for the purposes of national security, law enforcement, or public interests.

International Transfer of Data

We may choose to store your data on a local server or a server located in a foreign country depending upon the IT configurations of our websites and mobile applications, which may be adjusted from time to time. Where the transfer of your data to a server located in a foreign country satisfies the definition of international transfer or cross-border transfer of data under the applicable law, we will determine the legal basis for the transfer and take appropriate security measures to ensure a level of protection as required under the applicable law.

Data Retention

We will only retain your data for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil the purposes set out in this Policy. We will either delete or anonymise your data when it is no longer required to satisfy the purposes set out in this Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Policy, please contact our data protection department at dp-enquiries@usegis.co.uk.
